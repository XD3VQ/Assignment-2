The Leaf

  • The part that undergoes (the most obvious) life and death cycle, between calender seasons.
  • Serves as a way for a tree to absorb sunlight, more specifically vits and mins.
    • Other unique purposes include weather protection, insect deterrent, and the spread of seeds.
  • Depending on the type, can be suitable for human consumption and potentially beneficial for health. Same goes for a multitude of insects and animals alike.
